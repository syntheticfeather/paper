运算律
交换律
A . B = B . A
A  + B = B  + A
集合律
A  + (B  + C) = (A  + B)  + C
A . B . C = (A . B) . C = A . (B . C) = (A . C) . B
分配律
A . (B  + C) = (A . B)  + (A . C)
证明:
    A . (B  + C) = (A . B)  + (A . C)
    A . (B  + C) = A . B  + A . C
    A . B  + A . C = A . B  + A . C
    A . B  + A . C = (A . B)  + (A . C)
    所以，分配律成立。
